My Dream Place

Site Purpose

"My Dream Place" helps travelers discover, plan, and book their dream destinations effortlessly, making every journey unforgettable.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: The Dream Planner

Visitor: Sarah, a 28-year-old professional who has always dreamed of visiting Bali but doesn’t know where to start.

Question: I want to visit Bali, but I’m overwhelmed by all the options. How do I plan the perfect trip that fits my budget and interests?

Content Needs:

  • A step-by-step travel planning guide for Bali.
  • Budget-friendly tips and packages.
  • Recommendations for must-visit spots, accommodations, and activities.
  • User reviews and testimonials from others who have traveled to Bali.

Scenario 2: The Adventurous Explorer

Visitor: Mike, a 35-year-old adventure enthusiast.

Question: "Where can I find unique, off-the-beaten-path destinations?"

Content Needs:

  • A curated list of hidden gem destinations.
  • Tips for sustainable and responsible travel.
  • Interactive maps or itineraries for unique locations.
  • Adventure travel guides (e.g., hiking, diving, or cultural experiences).

Color Scheme

Primary Color: Deep Ocean Blue (#005F73) – Represents trust, calm, and exploration.

Secondary Color: Sunny Yellow (#FFBA08) – Evokes energy, optimism, and adventure.

Neutral Colors:

  • Light Gray (#F2F2F2) – For backgrounds and clean design.
  • Dark Gray (#333333) – For text and headings.

Typography

Google Fonts

  • Headings: "Poppins" (Semi-bold, 600) – A modern sans-serif font for a sleek and professional look.
  • Body Text: "Bebas" (Regular, 400) – Modern and impactful.
  • "Abril Fatface" (Bold and elegant).

Web-Safe Fonts

  • Impact (Strong and attention-grabbing).
